Physicae

by Casper

This page contains a list of every formula I use throughout my courses in order of appearance.

Newton's Laws of Motion

$$p=mv$$ $$F=ma$$

3 Equations of Motion

$$v=u+at$$ $$s=ut+\frac{1}{2}at^{2}$$ $$v^{2}-u^{2}=2as$$

Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation

$$F=G\frac{m_{1}m_{2}}{r^{2}}$$ $$F=gm$$

Work and Energy

$$W=F·s·cos(θ)$$ $$W=\Delta E_{k}$$

Forms of Energy

$$K=\frac{1}{2}mv^{2}$$ $$F_{s}=k⋅x$$ $$U=\frac{1}{2}kx^{2}$$ $$U=-\frac{GMm}{r}$$ $$U=mgh$$ $$E_{mechanical}=U+K$$

Pressure

$$p=\frac{F}{A}$$ $$p=\frac{E}{V}$$ $$p_{hydro}=ρgh$$

The Bernoulli Principle

$$p_{1}+\frac{1}{2}ρv_{1}^{2}+ρgy_{1}=p_{2}+\frac{1}{2}ρv_{2}^{2}+ρgy_{2}$$

Mass Flow

$$f=Av$$ $$A_{1}v_{1}ρ_{1}=A_{2}v_{2}ρ_{2}$$ $$A_{1}v_{1}=A_{2}v_{2}$$
