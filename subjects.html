My website covers a wide arrange of subjects, from physics to maths to ethics. Click on the button below a subject to go to a list of its courses. Read the descriptions to find out what interests you most.

Physics

This website started out being only about physics. At the time it was called Physicae. I enjoyed making those courses, which is why I decided to start adding more subjects. Physics covers everything from Newton's laws of motion to nuclear decay and quarks. This is the subject with the most self-researched courses.

Chemistry

Chemistry is probably my third favorite subject at school, only behind maths and physics. Chemistry covers everything from the composition of orbitals to the periodic table to reaction rates. This is mainly school curriculum translated and turned into neat online courses.

Biology

I'm going to be completely honest, I don't like biology. I don't hate it either, but it doesn't interest me as much as the other main sciences. Biology covers everything from how animals behave to the composition of cells and the replication of DNA.

Geography

Geography became cool in recent years, because they stopped talking about farmers and poor people, and finally got to the space shit. Geography covers everything from space to geology to industrialization.

Maths

Maths is my second favorite subject. I've always been quite good at it, because it's logical and I love playing with numbers. Maths covers everything from trigonometry to derivatives to complex numbers.

Ethics

You didn't expect ethics to be a subject here, did you? For some reason - even I don't know why - I really like thinking about ethical problems (and arguments between my friends) and how to solve them. I've come to form my personal view on the world and how problems should be dealt with. That's what I want to discuss here. Prepare for some very subjective courses!
