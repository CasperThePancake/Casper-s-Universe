Ypsylon

Ypsylon is a conlang I created from the ground up. It is based on Latin, Greek, Dutch, English, and French. It uses a case system, has a vocabulary list, multiple tenses, and a full story written in it. As of right now, the only documentation available is in Dutch. An English translation might come in the future.
